viewDidDisappear->viewDidAppear；

viewDidAppear->viewDidDisappear；
//pop、dismiss
3.返回第一个vc：viewWillDisappear->viewWillAppear->viewDidDisappear->viewDidDisappear
->dealloc；//不重新创建第一个vc，第二个vc销毁
